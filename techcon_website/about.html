History

TechCon began in 2010 as a grassroots initiative to unite developers and technologists. Over the years, it has grown into a global platform for innovation and collaboration.

Mission

TechCon's mission is to inspire and empower the tech community through open knowledge, groundbreaking talks, and inclusive networking opportunities.
